WAYS TO HELP PREVENT LEAD POISONING.

Hover over the image to see the description of each way you can take action to prevent the negative effects of lead poisoning from harming your physical health as well as the future's health.

practice safe paint encapsulation. peeling paint is a large culprit in mobilized lead chips.

if your soil is lead contaminated, apply at least an inch of new, healthy soil over the contaminated soil.
if the soil tested in your garden is high in lead content, restrict as much contact with the soil as possible and talk to your local health department.
